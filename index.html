About Me

I am a second-year undergraduate student in Artificial Intelligence at Harbin Institute of Technology. I work with Prof. Shuo Yang in the HITSZ M-PAI Lab.

My research interests include Humanoid robot learning and control, Reinforcement Learning, and Out-of-Distribution Detection.

My ambition is to make AI the interface through which human wisdom expands reality.

Research Interests

  • Humanoid robot learning and control: whole-body control and loco-manipulation.
  • Reinforcement Learning: sequential decision-making, exploration, and policy learning.
  • OOD Detection: robust recognition of samples outside the training distribution.
